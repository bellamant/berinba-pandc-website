Get Involved

Volunteer at the Canteen

Our canteen runs entirely on parent volunteers. Even one session a term helps enormously.

⏰

Shift Times

Canteen volunteers are needed from 8:30am to 1:30pm on canteen days. You're welcome to stay for only part of the shift if that suits you better.

🎓

Training Provided

No experience needed! Our canteen coordinator will walk you through everything. NSW Food Safety basics are covered in a short induction.

📋

Working With Children Check

All canteen volunteers must hold a current NSW Working With Children Check (WWCC). It's free for volunteers — apply at service.nsw.gov.au.
