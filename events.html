Term 2 — 28 April to 4 July 2026 Weeks 1–10
6 May 2026

P&C General Meeting Meeting

Monthly P&C meeting open to all parents and community members. Agenda will include Term 2 canteen review, Fun Run planning update, uniform shop stock, and general business. Refreshments provided.

🕡 6:30pm – 8:00pm 📍 School Library 👥 All welcome · No RSVP required
9 May 2026

Mother's Day Stall Fundraiser

Students visit the stall before school to select a gift for a special person in their life. Gifts are beautifully wrapped and ready to take home. All items are priced so students can buy with a gold coin or small amount of pocket money.

🕗 8:30am – 9:00am 📍 School Hall (Undercover Area) 💰 Items from $2–$8
📝 Volunteers needed! Email pandc@berinbaps.com.au to help set up and run the stall.
23 May 2026

School Fun Run 2026 Community

Our major Term 2 fundraiser! All students are encouraged to collect sponsorships and run as many laps as they can around the oval. Prizes for top fundraisers and top runners in each year group. Fancy dress welcome!

🕙 10:00am – 11:30am 📍 School Oval 👧👦 All students

Sponsorship forms available from the school office from Week 2. Funds raised go towards new playground equipment for the junior school.

3 Jun 2026

P&C General Meeting Meeting

June P&C meeting. Agenda to include Fun Run debrief and funds raised, Term 3 planning, and any other business. All welcome!

🕡 6:30pm – 8:00pm 📍 School Library 👥 All welcome
11 Jun 2026

Scholastic Book Fair Fundraiser

The Scholastic Book Fair returns for Term 2! Students can browse and purchase books during lunch breaks throughout the week. A wonderful opportunity to foster a love of reading.

📅 11–13 June (3 days) 📍 School Library 🕐 Recess & Lunch breaks
