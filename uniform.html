Second-Hand Uniforms

♻️ Pre-Loved Uniforms

We stock a selection of quality second-hand uniforms sold at a fraction of the original price. All items are clean and in good condition.

Second-hand items are available on a first-come, first-served basis at the Friday morning uniform shop opening.

📦 Donate Outgrown Uniforms

Has your child outgrown their uniform? Please donate clean, good-condition items to the uniform shop — another family will thank you!

Drop donations into the school office at any time.

Help

Frequently Asked Questions

❓ Payment Methods

We accept cash and card at the Wednesday morning shop. Exact change is appreciated for cash transactions.

❓ Can I Try Before I Buy?

Yes! We have fitting samples at the shop. If a size doesn't fit, items in original condition can be exchanged within 30 days.

❓ What if the Shop is Closed?

Email us at uniform@berinbapandc.org or ask at the school office. We can usually arrange to have items available for pickup.
