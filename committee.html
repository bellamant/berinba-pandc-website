Get Involved

Join the P&C

🙋 How to Join

Anyone with an interest in Berinba Public School can join the P&C — you don't need to have children at the school. Just come along to any P&C meeting and introduce yourself!

Annual membership is just $2, which gives you full voting rights at all meetings. Membership forms are available at the school office or at any P&C meeting.

📅 When We Meet

P&C meetings are held on the second Tuesday of each month at 7:00pm in the Staff Room.
